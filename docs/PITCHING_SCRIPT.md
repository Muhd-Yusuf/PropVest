# PropVest — Pitching Script

> This is your talking script. Read it, practice it, own it.
> You don't need to memorize word-for-word. Just know the story.
> Each section is a "scene" — like telling a friend a story.

---

## THE KEY MINDSET — READ THIS FIRST

> Before you say a single word to anyone, understand this:

**PropVest is NOT the brand. The developer IS the brand.**

People are not investing in PropVest. They are investing in Saleem Goje's property. They are investing in Dan Mama's duplex. Your platform is invisible. It is the engine, not the car.

Think of it like this: When you buy something on Paystack, you don't say "I bought from Paystack." You say "I bought from the shop." Paystack just processed the payment. Nobody cares about Paystack. They care about the shop.

That is PropVest. We are the Paystack of real estate investment. The developer's name is on everything. The developer's Instagram brings the followers. The developer's trust sells the units. We just handle the boring stuff — payments, KYC, payouts, legal.

**So when you pitch, you are NOT saying "we will market your properties for you."**

You are saying: **"Your followers already want to invest in your properties. We give you the tool to let them do it."**

Big difference.

---

## OPENING (30 seconds)

> Start with this. Don't introduce yourself yet. Start with the question.

"Oga, let me ask you something.

You post a property on Instagram. Beautiful duplex in Gwarinpa. N90 million. Your comments are full of people saying 'Masha Allah, I wish I can afford this.'

100,000 people saw that post. 50,000 liked it. But how many can actually buy? Maybe 5 people in this whole country can write a cheque for N90 million.

But what if — every single one of those 50,000 followers could buy a piece of that property? Not the whole thing. Just a small piece. N100,000 each.

50,000 people at N100,000 is N5 billion. You only need 900 of them to fully fund one duplex.

You already have the audience. You already have the trust. What you don't have is the system to collect N100,000 from 900 people, track who owns what, and pay them when it is time — whether that is quarterly rent or profit when you sell.

That is what I built."

---

## SCENE 1: THE PROBLEM (2 minutes)

> Now explain the pain. Use real examples they can relate to.

"Let me show you what is happening right now.

Your follower Aisha — she is a teacher. She earns N200,000 a month. She sees your properties every day on Instagram. She wants to invest in real estate so badly. But your cheapest property is N25 million. She will never afford it.

So what does she do? She puts her money in PiggyVest. Or she buys dollar stocks on Risevest. Good platforms. But she WANTED real estate. She wanted YOUR property. She just couldn't afford the entry price.

Now multiply Aisha by 100,000. That is your following on Instagram. 100,000 people who trust you, who love your work, who would invest in your property TODAY — if only the price was small enough.

Right now, all those people are just likers and commenters. They give you engagement but not money. What if you could turn every single follower into an investor?"

> Pause. Then hit the second pain:

"Now look at the other side. Your side.

You want to build a new estate. You need N500 million. You go to the bank — 25% interest. That is N125 million in interest alone. Robbery.

Or you sell off-plan. But you have to find 10-15 rich buyers willing to pay N30-50 million each. That takes months. Sometimes a whole year. And while you wait, construction is paused, your contractors are idle, and money is burning.

But you have 100,000 followers who trust you. What if you could raise N500 million from 5,000 of them at N100,000 each? In weeks, not months. No bank. No interest. No chasing rich buyers."

---

## SCENE 2: THE SOLUTION (2 minutes)

> Explain the idea simply. Keep it about THEIR brand, not yours.

"Here is what I am proposing.

You have a property — could be a duplex for rent, could be a new estate you are building to sell. We split it into small units at N100,000 each. Your followers buy those units.

You post on your Instagram: 'Invest in my property. Own a piece from N100,000.' Your followers click the link. They land on YOUR branded page. Not PropVest's page. YOUR page. It says 'Saleem Goje Properties.' Your logo. Your name. Your trust.

They sign up, verify their bank account, and buy units. The money goes straight to YOUR bank account via Paystack — not our account. We charge the investor a small platform fee on top, so you receive the full amount. You see every kobo land in your own bank app.

That is it. Now here is where it gets interesting.

**If it is a rental property** — like your duplex in Gwarinpa, N90 million. We split it into 900 units. That duplex is rented out. N4.2 million per year. That rent gets split among all unit holders every quarter. Automatically. Straight to their bank accounts via Paystack. Aisha the teacher — she bought 5 units for N500,000. She gets about N5,000 every quarter. She just invested in YOUR property and sat down.

**If it is a build-and-sell property** — like a new estate you are building. You need N250 million. Instead of going to the bank at 25% interest, 2,500 followers fund the construction at N100,000 each. You build the estate. You sell the houses. Total sale price maybe N350 million. That N100 million profit gets shared with your investors. Musa invested N500,000 — he gets N700,000 back. 40% return in 12-18 months. No rent, no waiting — just straight profit when you sell.

**Either way** — your followers become your investors. Your investors become your biggest fans. They will share YOUR property listings because they literally own a piece. Free marketing from people who have skin in the game."

> If they ask about crypto or blockchain:

"No crypto. No blockchain. Just Naira. Bank transfer in. Bank transfer out. As simple as buying airtime."

## SCENE 3: WHAT WE ACTUALLY DO (1 minute)

> This is where you explain PropVest's role clearly. We are the engine, not the face.

"So what does PropVest actually do? Think of us as your back office.

You are the face. Your brand. Your Instagram. Your trust. Your properties. That is all you.

We handle the boring stuff that you don't want to deal with:

One — the technology. The app, the website, the investor dashboard. We build and maintain all of that.

Two — KYC. Every investor gets verified with BVN and NIN. We handle that so you don't have to chase anyone for documents.

Three — payments. We integrate Paystack so people can pay with bank transfer, card, or USSD. We track every kobo.

Four — legal. We handle the contracts, investor agreements, and all the paperwork.

Five — payouts. When rent comes in, we calculate each investor's share and send it to their bank account automatically. Every quarter. You don't have to do the math or send individual transfers.

Six — secondary market. If an investor wants to sell their units, they can sell to another investor on the platform. We handle that too.

You just do what you already do — build properties, manage tenants, collect rent. We handle everything else."

---

## SCENE 4: WHY THIS WORKS FOR YOU (2 minutes)

> Talk directly to the developer about what they gain.

"Now let me tell you why this is a no-brainer for you.

**Number one — you unlock your audience.**
You have 100,000+ followers. Right now, 99% of them can't buy from you because your properties are too expensive. With this system, every single follower becomes a potential investor. You are not looking for new customers. You are activating the ones you already have.

**Number two — money comes faster.**
Instead of waiting for one person to pay N90 million — which could take months — you get 900 people paying N100,000 in weeks. Your followers are already warm. They trust you. They just need the link.

**Number three — you keep your stake.**
You don't have to sell all the units. If only 600 out of 900 sell, the remaining 300 are yours. For rental properties, you earn rent on those units. For build-and-sell, you keep your share of the profit. You are not losing anything.

**Number four — your followers market for you.**
When Aisha buys 5 units in your duplex, what does she do? She tells her friends. She posts about it. 'I just invested in Saleem Goje property!' That is free marketing. And those friends come and buy too. Your investors become your salesforce.

**Number five — you stay in control.**
You choose which properties to list. You set the price. If it is rental — you manage the tenant, collect the rent. If it is build-and-sell — you build, you sell. Nothing changes about your business. You just have a new channel to raise capital.

**Number six — you get a dashboard.**
You log in and see everything — how many units sold, how much raised, who your investors are, payout schedules. Full transparency."

## SCENE 5: LAND INVESTMENT (1 minute)

> Only mention this if they do land development.

"This works for land too.

You have 2.5 hectares in Kuje. You are selling plots at N7,000 per square meter.

Right now, you need 50 people to buy full plots. That is 50 rich people. Hard to find.

With this system, someone can buy just 50 square meters for N350,000. Another person buys 200 square meters. As plots sell — 25%, 50%, 75% — investors get milestone payouts from the profit.

And here is the sweet part — if someone accumulates 500 square meters, they can convert to a full physical plot with deed of assignment in their name.

So your follower who could never afford a full plot can invest small-small and eventually own one. That is powerful. They will love you for it."

---

## SCENE 6: THE MONEY (1 minute)

> Be transparent about how you make money. This is the updated fee model.

"How do we make money? Very simple and very transparent.

**Revenue stream one — Investment fee.** When an investor buys units, they pay a small platform fee on top. So on N100,000 worth of units, the investor pays N102,000. The extra N2,000 is ours. You get your full N100,000.

**Revenue stream two — Platform fee on payouts.** When rent comes in or a property sells, we take a small percentage before distributing to investors. So if N1 million in rent is collected, we take our cut, you take your management fee, and the rest goes to investors.

**Revenue stream three — Trading fee.** When someone sells their units on the secondary market, there is a 1% trading fee, capped at N5,000 per trade. So even on a N10 million trade, the maximum fee is N5,000. We don't want investors to feel robbed.

Now here is the important part about YOUR earnings:

**You set a management fee per property.** This is your compensation for managing the property — handling tenants, maintenance, all that work you already do. You decide the percentage. So when rent comes in, your management fee is taken first, then our platform fee, then the rest goes to investors. And if you keep some units unsold — you earn payouts on those units too, just like any other investor.

That is it. You don't pay us anything upfront. We only make money when investors invest. All Paystack transaction fees? We absorb those. The investor never sees a Paystack charge — just the platform fee."

> This is where you tell them what you want.

"Here is what I am proposing.

Let us start with just ONE property. Pick any one. We connect your bank account, build your branded page, and you post the link on your Instagram.

You don't pay anything. No listing fee for the first property. Zero risk for you.

If your followers invest — and they will, because they already trust you — then we both win. You got your capital faster. I proved my platform works.

Then we do the second property. Then the third. Then your whole portfolio.

Let's just test it with one."

**If talking to an investor:**

"I need N15-20 million to build the platform, handle legal setup, and sign our first developer partner.

The market is N59 trillion. There are 200 million Nigerians and zero platforms doing Naira-based fractional real estate with trusted developers. We have developer conversations in progress.

Year 1: 10 properties, 1,200 investors, N450M managed. Year 3: 80 properties, 20,000 investors, N7.5B managed, N337M revenue."

## HANDLING QUESTIONS

> People will ask questions. Here are the answers.

---

**"But we already sell our properties fine. Why do we need this?"**

"You sell to rich people. 5-10 buyers per property. This opens you to 100,000+ buyers you've never been able to reach. It's not replacing your current sales — it is a new revenue channel on top of what you already do."

---

**"Why can't I just build this myself?"**

"You can. But do you want to? Do you want to handle KYC for 10,000 investors? Do you want to build payment systems? Do you want to calculate and distribute rent to 5,000 bank accounts every quarter? That is not your business. Your business is building properties. Let us handle the technology and you focus on what you do best."

---

**"What if the property doesn't sell all units?"**

"No problem at all. You keep the unsold units. You are just another investor in your own property. You earn rent on your units just like everyone else. There is zero pressure to sell everything."

---

**"What if the tenant stops paying rent?"**

"Same thing that happens normally. You manage the tenant. If they default, you find a new one. Nothing changes about how you run your property. The only difference is that instead of one owner waiting for rent, there are many — and the risk is shared."

---

**"What if an investor wants their money back?"**

"Three ways. One — sell units on the marketplace to another investor. Two — hold and keep collecting rent. Three — if the property is sold, everyone gets their share. Nobody's money is locked forever."

---

**"What about properties I build to sell, not rent?"**

"It works exactly the same way. Let's say you are building a 10-unit estate for N250 million. We split that into 2,500 units at N100,000 each. Your followers fund the construction. You build. You sell. When the houses sell for N350 million, that N100 million profit gets distributed to all unit holders based on how many units they own. No rent calculations — just straight profit sharing when you sell."

---

**"Is this legal?"**

"Yes. Every investor signs a legal investment agreement. Each property has proper contracts — your partnership agreement, investor certificates, everything documented. We also register with the relevant authorities. This is standard globally. REITs work the same way. We are just making it accessible."

---

**"How do I know you won't steal the money?"**

"The money never touches our account at all. When an investor pays, Paystack sends the investment amount straight to YOUR bank account via a dedicated virtual account. Our platform fee is charged separately to the investor on top — so you always receive the full unit price. You can open your bank app right now and watch the money come in. We never hold your money. Full transparency."

---

**"What about the economy? Naira is falling."**

"That is actually WHY people should invest in property. When naira falls, property prices go UP in naira. Real estate is the best inflation hedge in Nigeria. Your followers' money is safer in your property than in a savings account earning 5% while inflation is 30%."

---

**"What if nobody invests?"**

"Look at your Instagram. How many people comment 'I wish I could afford this'? Those people WANT to invest. The only reason they don't is price. We just lowered the price to N100,000. They will come. And if they don't — you lost nothing. You still have your property. Zero risk."

---

**"How is this different from PiggyVest or Risevest?"**

"PiggyVest is savings. Risevest is dollar stocks. Neither gives you Nigerian real estate. We are the only platform where someone can own a piece of a verified Nigerian property, earn rent in Naira, and sell anytime. Different market entirely."

---

## PRACTICE TIPS

1. **Practice out loud.** Not in your head. Stand up and say it to a mirror or record yourself on your phone.

2. **8-10 minutes max.** Don't talk too long. Say the story. Stop. Let them ask questions.

3. **Start with the Instagram question.** "You post a property, 50,000 people like it, but how many can actually buy?" That hooks them.

4. **Use THEIR name.** If you are talking to Saleem Goje, say "Saleem Goje" in the examples. "When someone invests in Saleem Goje's property..." Make it personal.

5. **Don't say "we do your marketing."** Say "YOUR followers, YOUR audience, YOUR trust." PropVest is invisible. They are the star.

6. **Pause after big numbers.** "900 people at N100,000 each." Stop. Let it sink in.

7. **If you don't know the answer — say so.** "Great question. Let me get back to you on that." Better than lying.

8. **Bring the pitch deck on a tablet.** When you talk about the app or the dashboard, show them the screens. People trust what they can see.

9. **End with "just one property."** Don't ask for 10. Don't ask for their whole portfolio. Just one. Low commitment. Easy yes.

10. **Follow up same day.** WhatsApp after the meeting: "Thank you for your time sir. Here is the pitch deck for your review. Whenever you are ready to pick the first property, I am here." Then attach the PDF.
